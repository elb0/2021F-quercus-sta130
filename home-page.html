Though we are all online, we wish to acknowledge the land on which the University of Toronto operates. For thousands of years, it has been the traditional land of the  Huron-Wendat , the  Seneca , and most recently, the  Mississaugas of the Credit River . Today, this meeting place is still the home to many Indigenous people from across Turtle Island and we are grateful to have the opportunity to base our teaching and learning from on this land.

We encourage you to consider the history of the land wherever you are now. https://www.whose.land/en/
